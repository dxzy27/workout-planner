Bodyweight High-Intensity Workout
========================================

              MONDAY
---------------------------------

1. 3x12 Jump Squats
2. 3x10 Push-Ups
3. 3x8 Burpees

========================================

              TUESDAY
---------------------------------

1. 3x20 seconds High Knees
2. 3x10 Side Lunges (per leg)
3. 3x10 Plank with Shoulder Taps (per side)

========================================

            WEDNESDAY
---------------------------------

1. 3x8 Broad Jumps
2. 3x6-8 Plyometric Push-Ups (or Clap Push-Ups)
3. 3x25 seconds Mountain Climbers

========================================

             THURSDAY
---------------------------------

1. Active Recovery: Stretching or light yoga

========================================

              FRIDAY
---------------------------------

1. 3x8 Jumping Lunges (per leg)
2. 3x8 Burpees with Jump
3. 3x8 Side Plank with Leg Lifts (per side)

========================================

             SATURDAY
---------------------------------

1. 20 seconds on, 40 seconds rest x 5 rounds Sprint Intervals
2. 3x10 Jump Squats
3. 3x12 Plank-to-Knee Taps

========================================

              SUNDAY
---------------------------------

1. Recovery Day: Light stretching

========================================
